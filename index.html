What we install

Flake systems and metallic epoxies.

Flake systems

Flake Epoxy Systems

Decorative vinyl flake broadcast into 100% solids epoxy with anti-slip aggregate as needed, sealed with polyurea/polyaspartic. Flakes hide concrete imperfections and provide slip resistance — we do not recommend solid color on older garage floors.

Metallic epoxies

Metallic Epoxy

Metallic pigments floated into the resin create a deep, one-of-a-kind marbled finish. A showpiece option for garages and commercial spaces that want something beyond standard flake.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

We've put together some of our most frequently asked questions. These might help you understand what we do a little better.

What are epoxy floors?
Epoxy floors are polymer flooring systems created by combining chemical ingredients applied to concrete that harden or “cure.” The result is durable, chemically resistant, easy to clean, anti-slip, and anti-dusting. We use industrial/professional grade coatings — not DIY paint kits from Depot stores that peel within 1–2 years.
How long will it take?
This is a 2 to 3 day project. Day 1: degrease, etch, repairs, primer, epoxy with decorative chips. Day 2: polyurea/polyaspartic topcoat — the #1 topcoat on the market. Day 3 only if weather/dampness requires extra cure time or a moisture vapor barrier is used.
How long before it will be dry?
In 48 hours, the floor will be dry enough to place items back into the garage. It takes 72 hours before it is safe to park a car on the floor.
What do I need to do to prepare?
The garage must be empty upon arrival — due to insurance reasons we can’t move items. We can help dolly a disconnected fridge or washer/dryer outside the garage door only. Water softeners — we work around them. Items on shelves or overhead racks are not a problem.
Why go with a flake system rather than solid color?
We do NOT recommend solid color epoxy for garages — only exception is a brand new build with pristine concrete. Solid color shows every imperfection and is very slippery. Flake hides imperfections and acts as a slip resist. We will not do solid color on older garage floors riddled with cracks.
Other companies say they can do an epoxy job in 1 day?
Watch out! One-day products are designed to dry quicker than normal 100% solid epoxy — cutting corners on labor. These products are cheaper than what we use, yet some contractors charge more. We allow proper cure times and use primer, 100% solids epoxy, and polyurea topcoat.
