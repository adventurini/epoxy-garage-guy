How a floor gets done

Proper prep. Industrial products. Lasting results.

1

Grind & repair

Diamond-grind the slab, fill cracks, and prep the surface so the coating bonds properly.

2

Primer & base coat

We use primer and 100% solids industrial epoxy — not the low-end kits sold in big-box stores.

3

Flake or metallic

Broadcast flake or float metallic pigment to create your chosen look.

4

Topcoat

Seal everything with a clear polyaspartic topcoat for gloss, protection, and long-term durability.
